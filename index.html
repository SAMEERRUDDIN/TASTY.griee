Welcome to the TASTY food world

Established in November of 2019, The TASTY Restaurant has quickly achieved respect and success within the region, rising to the number one position on OpenTable among all metropolitan Baton Rouge restaurants.
Chef sameer ruddin and his team have found an ideal balance between Cajun culture, European tradition, and cutting edge flavors that the public can’t get enough of.
Nothing pleases us more than to hear over and over again, “That was the best meal I’ve ever had”.
